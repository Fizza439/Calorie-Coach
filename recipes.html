Breakfast

Anda Paratha Roll

Recipe: Prepare a whole wheat paratha and fill it with a spiced egg omelette. Roll it up and serve with yogurt dip.

Benefits:

Rich in protein and carbs, this breakfast provides sustained energy for a productive morning.

Multigrain Porridge

Recipe: Cook a blend of oats, barley, and quinoa with milk. Top with dry fruits and a drizzle of honey.

Benefits:

High in fiber and nutrients, it supports digestion and keeps you full for longer.

Lunch

Grilled Chicken

Recipe: Marinate chicken in spices, grill until tender, and serve with steamed vegetables and whole wheat roti.

Benefits:

A high-protein meal that aids muscle growth and provides essential vitamins from vegetables.

Daal Chawal

Recipe: Cook lentils with onions, tomatoes, and spices. Serve with steamed basmati rice and a side salad.

Benefits:

A comforting and balanced meal rich in plant-based protein and complex carbs.

Recipe: Simmer chicken with carrots, celery, and onions in a flavorful broth. Add herbs for extra taste.

Snacks

Roasted Chickpeas

Recipe: Toss boiled chickpeas with olive oil, salt, and spices. Roast in the oven until crispy.

Benefits:

Rich in protein and fiber, this snack helps in weight management and keeps you energized.

Fresh Fruit Salad

Recipe: Chop seasonal fruits and toss them with a sprinkle of chaat masala.

Benefits:

A refreshing and hydrating snack packed with natural vitamins and antioxidants.
