Understanding the Metrics

This health calculator provides detailed insights into your body's health status:

  • BMI: Body Mass Index indicates your weight status.
  • BMR: Basal Metabolic Rate shows the calories burned at rest.
  • Body Fat: Calculates fat percentage based on body measurements.
  • Ideal Weight: Suggests a healthy weight range based on your height and gender.
  • Calories: Provides daily calorie needs based on your activity level.
