Exercises

Cardio

Benefits: Improves cardiovascular health, burns calories, and boosts mood.

Age Limit: All ages

Duration: 30-60 minutes

Equipment: None, or optional treadmill, jump rope, or stationary bike

Instructions: Engage in activities like brisk walking, jogging, cycling, or dancing. Maintain a steady pace to keep your heart rate elevated.

Strength Training

Benefits: Increases muscle mass, strengthens bones, and improves metabolism.

Age Limit: 18+

Duration: 20-40 minutes

Equipment: Dumbbells, resistance bands, or body weight

Instructions: Perform exercises like squats, lunges, push-ups, and deadlifts. Focus on controlled movements and proper form.

Flexibility

Benefits: Enhances range of motion, reduces injury risk, and improves posture.

Age Limit: All ages

Duration: 10-20 minutes

Equipment: Yoga mat (optional)

Instructions: Stretch major muscle groups, hold each stretch for 20-30 seconds, and avoid bouncing.

Balance

Benefits: Improves stability, coordination, and prevents falls.

Age Limit: All ages

Duration: 10-15 minutes

Equipment: None, or optional stability ball

Instructions: Try exercises like standing on one leg, heel-to-toe walking, or using a stability ball to challenge your balance.
